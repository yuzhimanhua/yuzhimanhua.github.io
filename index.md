## About Me
I am a Ph.D. student in the [Data Mining Group](http://dm1.cs.uiuc.edu) at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, advised by Prof. [Jiawei Han](http://hanj.cs.illinois.edu/). I finished my M.Sc. study in the same group in 2019. My research interests are text mining, text-rich network mining, and their applications to bioinformatics.

Prior to UIUC, I received my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from Peking University in 2017, advised by Prof. [Yan Zhang](http://www.cis.pku.edu.cn/jzyg/szdw/zy.htm). 

In summer 2021, I am interning at Microsoft Research, Redmond (virtually), working with Dr. [Iris Shen](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/zhihosh/).

In summer 2020, I interned at Microsoft Research, Redmond (virtually), working with Dr. [Iris Shen](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/zhihosh/) and Dr. [Yuxiao Dong](https://ericdongyx.github.io/).

In summer 2016, I visited Carnegie Mellon University, working with Prof. [Kathleen M. Carley](http://www.casos.cs.cmu.edu/bios/carley/carley.html).

For further information, please see my [CV](CV.pdf).
