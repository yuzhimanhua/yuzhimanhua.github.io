What you should know?

Tips for attending a virtual conference.
Get to know the virtual platform before the start of the conference.
Plan around your schedule. Don't be afraid to rely on recordings and on demand content.
Attend with others if you can. Get together with friends or colleagues.
Engage as much as possible. Being a virtual attendee doesn't mean you can't network.
